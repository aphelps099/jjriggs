Implements Playbook

Works Hard. Priced Right.

A value-focused implements campaign for Iron Craft and Braber attachments. No aggressive competitor attacks—just honest value and immediate availability.

01 — Campaign Positioning

The Core Message

"You need an implement that works as hard as you do without breaking the bank."
"And you need it TODAY."

This campaign speaks to the practical farmer/rancher who needs equipment that delivers value and is available NOW. No waiting lists, no premium pricing for "heirloom" status—just quality tools ready to work.

How This Differs from the Competitive Campaign

Previous: "Trophy vs. Tool"
Aggressive Competitive Response
Directly challenges competitor's "heirloom" positioning. Frames choice as identity (collector vs. operator). Edgy, confrontational tone targeting competitor customers.
New: "Works Hard. Priced Right."
Value & Availability Focus
Leads with YOUR value proposition—not attacks. Speaks to busy operators who need solutions now. Positive, confident, practical tone that builds trust.

Why this approach: Not everyone responds to competitive positioning. Many customers simply want to know: "Can you help me? Do you have what I need? Can I get it today?" This campaign answers those questions directly.

06 — Campaign Selection Guide

When to Use Which Campaign

Use "Works Hard. Priced Right." (this campaign) when:

  • Running general implements awareness campaigns
  • Targeting NEW customers who aren't comparing competitors
  • Emphasizing inventory availability
  • Building positive brand sentiment

Use "Trophy vs. Tool" competitive campaign when:

  • Running geofencing ads near competitor locations
  • Targeting customers actively comparing custom-welded options
  • Responding to competitor ads in the market
  • Retargeting users who've visited competitor sites

Both campaigns can run simultaneously to different audiences. The value-focused campaign builds your brand; the competitive campaign captures comparison shoppers.

07 — Quick Start

Launch Checklist

  • Download carousel slide images (or screenshot from this page)
  • Create new campaign in Facebook Ads Manager
  • Set objective to "Traffic" with implements page destination
  • Define audience: 30-65, Stevens County +75mi, farming interests
  • Upload 5 carousel slides in order
  • Add copy from Section 04 examples
  • Set link to jjriggsequipment.com/implements
  • Set daily budget at $25-40
  • Enable "Advantage+ Placements"
  • Publish and monitor after 48 hours
