How "geofencing" works on Facebook: Not true device-level geofencing — it's radius-based. Drop a pin, set a radius (1–50 mi). Facebook targets people who live in, recently visited, or are traveling through that area.

Launch Plan, Retargeting & Tracking

Everything else you need to go live and measure results.

5-Week Launch Sequence

W1
Setup
Install Meta Pixel. Film video b-roll. Take product photos. Build campaigns in draft.
W2
Geofencing + Carousel go live
Launch geofencing (competitors first). Launch carousel. Monitor daily.
W3
Video goes live
All three campaigns running. Monitor daily.
W4
Optimize
Kill underperformers. Scale winners +20%. Build retargeting audiences.
W5
Expand
Launch retargeting. Refresh creative monthly. Test new angles.

Retargeting Funnel

Video viewers (25%+ watched)
→ Show carousel ad. 30-day window.
Page / post engagers
→ Testimonial or customer story. 60-day window.
Website visitors
→ "Come back" ad + seasonal offer. 30-day window.
Carousel clickers (no conversion)
→ Specific model + financing info. 14-day window.
