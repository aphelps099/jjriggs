Playbook

12-Issue Newsletter Strategy

A year-long content calendar for monthly emails that build trust, stay relevant, and drive sales—without overwhelming subscribers.

01 — Philosophy

The JJ Riggs Approach

One email per month. No spam. No fluff. Every message should feel like it's coming from a neighbor who happens to sell tractors—helpful, honest, and worth reading.

Core promise: "Once a month, we'll send you updates on new TYM tractors, equipment arrivals, and deals worth knowing about."

Important: Newsletter launch depends on establishing an email list first. Begin list building through website signups, in-store captures, and customer opt-ins before launching the first newsletter.

Content Pillars

Pillar 1
Inventory & Deals
What's new, what's on sale, financing offers. Direct sales content.
Pillar 2
Maintenance & Tips
Seasonal equipment care, how-tos, service reminders. Build authority.
Pillar 3
Local & Personal
Customer stories, Andrew's picks, community connection. Build trust.

06 — Sending Schedule

When to Send

Element Recommendation
Send Day Tuesday or Wednesday Mid-week performs best
Send Time 8:00 AM or 10:00 AM PST Morning = higher opens
Week of Month First or second week Avoid end-of-month
Frequency Once per month Never more than 2x

Consistency matters: Pick a day (like the first Tuesday of every month) and stick with it. Subscribers will come to expect it.

07 — Metrics to Track

What Success Looks Like

  • Open Rate: Target: 25%+ Industry average is ~20% for retail
  • Click Rate: Target: 3%+ Clicks on any link in the email
  • Unsubscribe Rate: Keep under 0.5% If higher, content may not match expectations
  • List Growth: Track monthly Aim for steady growth from website form
  • Reply Rate: Bonus metric Personal replies indicate strong engagement
