How to Use These Templates

Option 1: Screenshot - Just screenshot any template you like and post directly to Facebook/Instagram.

Option 2: Customize - Edit the text in the HTML, save, and screenshot your customized version.

Swap in your own photos: Replace the image URLs with photos from your phone or website.

  • Square posts (1:1) - Best for Facebook and Instagram feeds
  • Story format (9:16) - Best for Instagram/Facebook Stories and Reels covers
  • Keep the captions real and conversational - match the "2-minute document" strategy
  • Always add a real caption when posting - these graphics are the visual, your caption is the conversation
