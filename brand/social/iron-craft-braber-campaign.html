Competitive Response Campaign

Iron Craft & Braber

Facebook carousel ads countering competitor "heirloom" positioning. Three concepts, each with a different strategic angle.

Strategic Context

These ads respond to a competitor's campaign that positioned custom-welded attachments as "family heirlooms" worth premium prices and long wait times. Our counter-positioning doesn't attack quality (no credible evidence of issues). Instead, we reframe the conversation around practical value.

Key messages: On-lot availability (no 6-8 week wait), ROI focus ("tools that pay for themselves"), Severe-Duty industrial ratings, Tennessee steel manufacturing, and multiple brand options vs. one manufacturer.
