Project proposal for Andrew Alluis / JJ Riggs Equipment

The situation. JJ Riggs is carrying two tractor lines now — existing TYM inventory that needs to move, and the new Bad Boy Tractors lineup that rolls out over the next year. The homepage and interior pages need to serve both.

The priority. TYM is the shorter clock — discontinued models are already being cleared, and existing stock needs to convert before it sits. Bad Boy is the longer play — full 8-model lineup with a year runway and strong social support from Bad Boy HQ.

The goal. A homepage and navigation that drives quote requests for both lines, plus a new set of Bad Boy Tractor pages to capture the regional search demand as it grows.

Where The Site Stands Today

Quick audit of what's working, what isn't, and what needs to change.

  • Bad Boy Tractors isn't on the site at all — only Bad Boy Mowers is represented.
  • TYM page still lists some discontinued models that need to be cleared.
  • Homepage looks great but the sales messaging can be stronger — adding financing callouts, testimonials, and prominent quote CTAs will turn more of the existing traffic into real leads.
  • Tractor category, page layout, and navigation need reworking to accommodate two tractor brands cleanly.
  • Platform is solid — WordPress + Flatsome + WooCommerce (catalog mode) + Gravity Forms + Rank Math. We're refining, not rebuilding.

The New Homepage

Built to turn existing traffic into quote requests.

What Changes

Both tractor lines featured. Dedicated panels for TYM and Bad Boy. TYM up front to move current inventory, Bad Boy introduced as the new lineup.

Clear sales CTAs throughout. Get a Quote, See Inventory, Apply for Financing — visible on every section.

Trust signals. Customer testimonials, Google reviews, and local Colville roots up front.

Financing callout. Most tractor buyers need financing. Most current visitors don't know JJ Riggs offers it.

Tractor Pages & Navigation

Two brands, one clean structure.

How It's Organized

Tractors menu splits into TYM Tractors and Bad Boy Tractors. Each opens its own hub.

TYM hub — discontinued models cleared out, remaining lineup reorganized with quote CTAs.

Bad Boy hub — full 8-model lineup using one shared product template. Bad Boy HQ supplies all specs, photos, and brochures through their dealer portal — we package the approved content, we don't write from scratch.

Every page follows the same pattern: hero → specs → use cases → financing → quote form.

Protecting The 400 Monthly Clicks

Redirects keep the existing Google traffic flowing when we restructure.

What We Do

Before we touch anything, we map every URL that's currently ranking in Google. As we restructure, old URLs get pointed to the new ones so none of the current search traffic is lost. After launch, we monitor for two weeks to catch anything that slipped.
