Proposal — Phase 2

Bad Boy Tractors Launch
+ Homepage Refresh

Project proposal for Andrew Alluis / JJ Riggs Equipment

The opportunity. JJ Riggs just picked up Bad Boy Tractors — and right now there are zero Bad Boy Tractor dealers listed in Washington state. We can own the "Bad Boy Tractors Washington" search term before anyone else in the region shows up.

The problem. The current site doesn't mention Bad Boy Tractors anywhere. The navigation still references "Branson" in the URL from the old brand era. As TYM gradually offsets, we need the site to clearly communicate both tractor lines with strong calls to action.

The plan. A focused Bad Boy launch + homepage refresh that updates navigation, adds dedicated product pages, and builds search visibility for the new line — all within a project budget that realistically pays for itself with one or two tractor sales.

Project Details

Client JJ Riggs Equipment / Andrew Alluis
Contractor Aaron C Phelps Creative
Phase Phase 2 — Bad Boy Launch
Recommended Option $5,200
Estimated Timeline 4 – 5 weeks

Site Audit — What We're Working With

  • Nav still uses the legacy URL /branson-tym-tractors/ — leftover from the previous brand, hurts SEO and credibility.
  • No mention of Bad Boy Tractors anywhere on the site. Only Bad Boy Mowers is represented.
  • Homepage is visually strong but light on content — missing financing CTA, testimonials, and trust signals.
  • Footer has no sitemap links, which limits internal SEO and crawlability.
  • Platform is WordPress + Flatsome + WooCommerce (catalog mode) + Gravity Forms + Rank Math — good foundation. We're refining, not rebuilding.

Three Options

Option A — Essentials
$2,400

The minimum needed to get Bad Boy Tractors live and indexed.

2 – 3 weeks

  • Navigation overhaul — separate TYM and Bad Boy dropdowns, retire /branson-tym-tractors/ slug with proper 301 redirects
  • Homepage Bad Boy Tractors panel inserted between TYM and Implements
  • Two SEO pages — "Bad Boy Tractors in Washington State" + "TYM vs Bad Boy Compact Tractor Comparison"
  • Schema markup and meta tag updates for the new product line
  • Google Business Profile update with Bad Boy Tractors added as a category
Recommended
Option B — Launch

The Math

Why This Pays For Itself

A single Bad Boy compact tractor retails in the $12,000 – $25,000 range. If this project drives one tractor sale we wouldn't have captured otherwise, the Recommended option returns 2 – 5x the investment.

Bad Boy also offers co-op marketing reimbursement for qualifying dealer advertising. Once we confirm JJ Riggs' co-op eligibility and submit the right documentation, a meaningful portion of the launch ad creative and campaign spend is typically reimbursable.

Net investment after co-op reimbursement is often significantly lower than the sticker price. We'll handle the submission paperwork as part of the launch.

Scope Boundaries

— Dealer application paperwork for Bad Boy Tractors (assumed complete before launch).
