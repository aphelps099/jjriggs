Frequently asked questions

Is Bad Boy as good as John Deere or Kubota? For the price point, yes. Bad Boy uses the same engines (Kawasaki, Kohler, Yanmar), the same transmissions (Hydro-Gear), and the same implement standards (Cat I, standard PTO) as the major brands. The frames are heavier on most Bad Boy models than the residential equivalents from green or orange. Where Bad Boy wins is price-to-spec ratio. Where the major brands win is resale value and parts ubiquity in markets where Bad Boy doesn't have a dealer.

What is the best Bad Boy mower for 5 acres in NE Washington? The Bad Boy ZT Elite 54 or Maverick HD 54 with a 24–26 HP Kawasaki engine. The ZT Elite is the budget pick. The Maverick HD is the more durable pick. Both will handle 5 acres of mixed lawn and pasture without complaint.

Does Bad Boy make a diesel mower? Yes — the Renegade Diesel runs a Kubota three-cylinder diesel and is built for commercial-scale mowing. For most NE Washington property owners under 20 acres, a gas Rogue or Maverick HD is the better fit. Diesel makes sense when you are running 4+ hours a day, 5+ days a week.

Can I run a snow plow or blower on a Bad Boy zero-turn? Not really. Zero-turns are not designed for snow work — the weight distribution and tire setup are wrong, and most Bad Boy zero-turns do not have a front PTO or a three-point hitch. For snow, use a Bad Boy compact tractor (1025H, 4025, or 4035) with a front blade or rear blower. See Best Tractor for Snow Removal in Northeast Washington for the full setup guide.

How long do Bad Boy mowers last? A residential ZT Elite or Maverick will run 1,500–2,000 hours with normal homeowner maintenance. The commercial-grade Rogue and Renegade lineups will run 3,000+ hours with proper service intervals. The deck and frame outlast the engine on every Bad Boy we have sold.

Do you carry Bad Boy tractor implements? Yes — we stock and order IronCraft and Braber implements that are sized and rated for Bad Boy compact tractors. Brush hogs, finish mowers, box blades, post hole diggers, snow blades, and more. We will match the implement to your tractor and your property when you buy.
